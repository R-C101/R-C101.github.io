Retrieval-guided adversarial attacks.

RECAP — a novel approach to adversarial attacks on LLMs.

Rather than searching the prompt space from scratch, RECAP uses semantic similarity search to retrieve proven-successful attack patterns from a curated database — turning red-teaming into a retrieval problem and dramatically improving hit rate against modern guarded models.
