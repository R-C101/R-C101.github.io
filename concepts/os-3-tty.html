Research

rishit@portfolio:~$ arxiv show RECAP

arXiv:2601.15331 [cs.CL] · 2026

RECAP — Retrieval-Enhanced Circumvention of Aligned Policies

Rishit Chugh

We present a retrieval-augmented framework for adversarial attacks on large language models. Rather than crafting jailbreaks from scratch, RECAP performs semantic similarity search over a curated database of proven-successful attack patterns, retrieving and adapting the closest prior exploits to new targets — a compact, transferable approach to LLM red-teaming.
