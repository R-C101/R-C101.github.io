# About Race In Progress

**Race In Progress** is an advanced, browser-based racing game that combines realistic physics, competitive multiplayer, and intelligent AI, all running directly in your web browser. Built on modern web technologies, it aims to deliver a console-quality racing experience without the need for downloads or installations.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🏎️ Diverse Roster of Cars
Choose from 7 distinct vehicles, each with meticulously tuned physics to match their real-world counterparts:
- **Hatchback**: Front-wheel drive, nimble, and predictable.
- **Muscle V8**: Heavy, powerful, and ready to slide.
- **Sport Flat-six**: Rear-engine traction with classic oversteer characteristics.
- **Rally Turbo**: All-wheel drive, high-boost, and exceptional launch grip.
- **Supercar V12**: High downforce, immense power, and aerodynamic stability.
- **F1**: Unbelievable grip, massive downforce, and blistering speed.
- **God Car**: The ultimate fantasy vehicle with custom neon underglow and physics-defying performance.

### 🌍 Iconic Tracks
Race on beautifully rendered, accurate layouts of legendary circuits:
- **Nürburgring Nordschleife**: The legendary 20km "Green Hell."
- **Nürburgring GP**: The modern, technical grand prix layout.
- **Spa-Francorchamps**: Fast, flowing, and historic.
- **Suzuka Circuit**: The iconic figure-8 track.

### 🌐 Peer-to-Peer Multiplayer
Race against your friends with seamless, serverless multiplayer:
- **Instant Join**: Share a link or room code to jump straight into the action.
- **Race Modes**: Ghost cars during practice, solid collisions during active races.
- **Zero Configuration**: Uses WebRTC for direct, low-latency connections.

### 🧠 "Verstappen-Grade" AI
Don't want to race alone? Challenge our highly capable AI drivers:
- **Dynamic Pacing**: AI evaluates track limits, braking zones, and grip levels in real-time.
- **Smart Overtaking**: Avoids collisions and recovers from spin-outs intelligently.
- **Custom Racing Lines**: Pre-calculated optimal paths for every circuit.

### 🎮 Ultimate Controller Support
Experience the track through your hands:
- **PlayStation DualSense Support**: Native support including **Adaptive Triggers** via WebHID (feel the ABS pulse and brake resistance!).
- **Standard Gamepads**: Full support for Xbox and other controllers with tailored force-feedback rumble based on surface texture, slip angle, and impacts.
- **Mobile Touch Controls**: Responsive on-screen pedals and steering for racing on the go, with dedicated portrait and landscape layouts.

### ⚙️ Deep Physics & Driver Assists
Powered by the Rapier physics engine, the driving model bridges the gap between arcade and simulation:
- **Customizable Assists**: Toggle Anti-Lock Brakes (ABS) and Traction Control (TC) to suit your skill level.
- **Adjustable Brake Bias**: Fine-tune your setup mid-race (forward-bias for stability, rear-bias for rotation).
- **Realistic Tire Model**: Pacejka friction curves simulate realistic grip loss and recovery.

### 📊 Telemetry & HUD
- **Stats-for-Nerds**: Live graphing of wheel slip, G-forces, suspension travel, and pedal inputs.
- **Lap Timer**: Track your personal bests with persistent history.
- **Live Minimap**: Keep an eye on your opponents and the upcoming corners.

## 🚀 Get Behind the Wheel
Race In Progress is constantly evolving. Jump into a lobby, rev your engine, and see you at the finish line!
